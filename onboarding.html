Welcome to Chrome Link Selector!

Thank you for installing Chrome Link Selector! Here's what you can do with our extension:

  • Select multiple similar links with one click
  • Draw a rectangle to select links in an area
  • Open multiple selected links at once
  • Undo/redo your selections with keyboard shortcuts
